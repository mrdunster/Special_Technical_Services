K78160A Ground Clamp

Our ground clamp is made from cast aluminum since non-ferrous metals will not generate a spark when struck. Its heavy duty clamping force and unique binding points will ensure proper connection without slipping of the surface it is clamped to. It is rated to be used in temperature down to -40°F (-40°C). The K78160ASRS model includes a strain release feature.

Strain release cable is optional

#1 Contactor

Our #1 contactor is used when two-ball connections is already mounted on the object requiring grounding. The connector is made of rugged plastic. The two-ball connector is also available.

Model VUD Clamp

This plier-type aluminum clamp is designed specifically for drum and tote groundng and bonding.
