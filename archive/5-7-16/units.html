STS 300 Ground Monitoring System

The STS 300 Series Ground Monitoring System is designed to provide safe permissive controls for all transfer of hazardous materials where electrostatic charges present a danger of ignition. The unit includes a ground cable of various length and types that is clamped to the frame of the vessel and completes a monitored path to ground. Once a positive ground is established visual indication is displayed at the unit and dry contacts provide a permissive interlock with pump start controls.

Four power source voltages are available:

  • Model G2 - 120VAC
  • Model G4 - 240VAC
  • Model G12 - 12VDC
  • Model G24 - 24VDC

A choice of indicator bulbs are available:

  • Incandescent: 12VDC, 24VDC, 120VAC, 240VAC
  • LED: 12VDC, 24VDC, 120VAC

Options:

Deadman Control - Additional cable with deadman handle that ensures proper manned supervision of operation.

Retractable Reel - provides safe cable management for straight cable systems and extends the life of the cable by keeping it properly strored between uses.

STS ESDM G8 Portable Discharge Monitor

Easily and quickly establishes and verifies continuous grounding for safe loading and unloading of hazardous liquids. The monitor dissipates static charges as they are generated preventing static buildup and potentially catastrophic fire and explosions. It eliminates guessing. The monitor clearly indicate proper continuity for loading and unloading operations involving drums, tankers, rail car, and barges. The monitor is ideal for temporary transfer stations, when no line voltage is available, or when permanent grounding installation are not possible.

Benefits:

  • Easy to read. Face panel shows at a glance whether it is safe to transfer hazardous liquids.
  • Simple operation. No programming and no keys or buttons to push. Not even an on-off switch.
  • Reliable, long life. Durable construction includes heavy-duty case, oil and gas resistant coiled cables and rugged clamps.

Includes:

  • One red LED to indicate no ground connection, one green LED to indicate the unit is charging and one green LED to indicate when a proper ground connection is detected.
  • Two coiled cords extending up to 18 feet with isolated grounding clamps.
  • 120 Volt AC charger.
